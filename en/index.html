Frequently asked questions

How many people can play?

PonyRun supports exactly 2–4 racers, matching its four distinct pony characters.

Does everyone need a phone?

No. Place one phone flat in landscape and gather around it. Everyone watches and grabs items on the same screen.

Do I need to install anything?

No. PonyRun runs directly in a modern mobile browser. There is no app download, registration, or room setup.

Is PonyRun free?

Yes. PonyRun is free to play in your browser.
